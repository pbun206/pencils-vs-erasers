Pencils vs Erasers

a tower defense game

How to Play

Pick a tower, click to place it. Survive 6 waves. Good luck with that.

Towers

Pencil — Melee. Cheap. Will carry you harder than your group project partners ever did.

Pen — Ranged. The pencil's overachieving sibling who studied abroad.

Notebook — Can't attack. Just absorbs damage. The unpaid intern of your defense.

Highlighter — Hits every enemy in the lane. Finally, a tower with ambition.

Marker — 600 ink. You'll look at the price, look at your ink, and quietly select pencil again.

Ink Blob — One-time use nuke. Clears a lane. You only get one per game so use it wisely. Or panic-click it on wave 2. We won't judge.

Enemies

About 6 types. Pink erasers walk and erase. Standard.

Some come gift-wrapped. Nobody asked for this gift. The wrapping is extra armor.

White-out is a scouting unit. It walks across and leaves. Harmless. Don't bother targeting it.

Highlighter erasers are in this game. Which enemy? What does it do? We'll never tell. Think about it every time something weird happens.

Later waves introduce new enemy types. Our playtester quit during testing. He was also the developer. He still shipped it.

Tips

Clear enemies fast for a timeskip and ink bonus.

Notebooks block your own projectiles, so don't place them in front of pens.

The pencil case (trash) button is there because even the game knows you'll make bad decisions.

This game has no difficulty settings. This is the easy mode.
